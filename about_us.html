ABOUT THE MSU


Medical Statistics Unit (MSU) has been established in the Ministry of Health around 1960 and function under the Deputy Director General (Medical Services) of the Ministry of Health. MSU is responsible to provide accurate, unbiased, reliable and timely statistics related to health sector of the country. Technical support for collect, compile and analysis hospital statistics is provided by the staff of the Department of Census and Statistics. Medical Statistics Unit (MSU) collect inpatient, outpatient and clinic data, health staff and specialists data, maternal and dental data and bed capacity. MSU publishes the Annual Health Bulletin (AHB) for disseminating statistical information and other remarkable activities carried out by the various units, campaigns, branches of the Ministry of Health. Hospital data collected by MSU are immensely useful for health sector policy planners, researchers, students.

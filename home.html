MSU is responsible for providing accurate, unbiased, reliable and timely statistics related to the country's health sector. Technical support for collecting, compiling and analyzing hospital statistics is provided by the staff of the Department of Census and Statistics. The medical Statistics Unit (MSU) collects maternal data monthly, inpatient and outpatient data (including clinic data) quarterly and hospital bed capacity and health staff data annually. >> more

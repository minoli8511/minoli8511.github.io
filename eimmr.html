Electronic Indoor Morbidity and Mortality Record System (eIMMR)


Are you new to eIMMR? Create your new eIMMR account today!
eIMMR is the first electronic health information system in Sri Lanka. It is a web based electronic system implemented in MSU to capture morbodity and mortality data in curative centers of Sri Lamka. Currently it is implemented in 531 curative centers.
